# Enterprise AI Adoption Resources

A curated collection of resources for leaders navigating enterprise AI adoption — from strategy through to production deployment. Maintained by [Agathon](https://agathon.ai), an AI consultancy specialising in product development, technical due diligence, and leadership advisory.

> **Why this list?** Most enterprise AI guidance falls into two camps: vendor marketing dressed as thought leadership, or academic research disconnected from operational reality. This list prioritises resources grounded in actual implementation experience.

## Strategy & Readiness

- [State of AI Report](https://www.stateof.ai/) — Annual report covering AI research, industry, politics, and safety. Essential strategic context.
- [AI Index Report (Stanford HAI)](https://aiindex.stanford.edu/report/) — Comprehensive annual data on AI development, adoption, and policy trends.
- [McKinsey Global Survey on AI](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/quantumblack/our-insights/the-state-of-ai) — Annual survey tracking enterprise AI adoption patterns and value creation.
- [Sequoia Capital: AI's $600B Question](https://www.sequoiacap.com/article/ais-600b-question/) — Sober analysis of AI revenue vs infrastructure spend.
- [The Trillion Dollar AI Software Development Stack](https://a16z.com/the-trillion-dollar-ai-software-development-stack/)) — Foundational essay on where value accrues in AI product development.

## Product Development

- [Anthropic: Building Effective Agents](https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/building-effective-agents) — Practical patterns for building AI agent systems from one of the leading model providers.
- [Google PAIR: People + AI Guidebook](https://pair.withgoogle.com/guidebook/) — Human-centred design patterns for AI products.
- [Lenny Rachitsky: How AI is Changing Product Management](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/) — Practitioner-focused insights on AI product development.
- [Marty Cagan: Product Discovery with AI](https://www.svpg.com/) — How AI changes product discovery and validation processes.
- [Eugene Yan: Patterns for Building LLM-based Systems](https://eugeneyan.com/writing/llm-patterns/) — Technical patterns from an experienced ML practitioner.

## LLMs & Foundation Models

- [Anthropic Documentation](https://docs.anthropic.com/) — Claude model capabilities, prompt engineering, and integration guides.
- [OpenAI Cookbook](https://cookbook.openai.com/) — Practical code examples for working with LLMs.
- [Hugging Face: Open LLM Leaderboard](https://huggingface.co/spaces/open-llm-leaderboard/open_llm_leaderboard) — Benchmarking open-source language models.
- [Simon Willison's Weblog](https://simonwillison.net/) — Arguably the best ongoing commentary on practical LLM usage and capabilities.
- [Latent Space Podcast](https://www.latent.space/) — Deep technical conversations on AI/ML engineering.
- [Vicki Boykis: What Are Embeddings?](https://vickiboykis.com/what_are_embeddings/) — Excellent technical primer on one of the most important concepts in applied AI.

## AI Agents

- [LangChain/LangGraph Documentation](https://python.langchain.com/docs/langgraph) — Framework for building stateful, multi-step AI agent workflows.
- [CrewAI Documentation](https://docs.crewai.com/) — Multi-agent orchestration framework.
- [Microsoft AutoGen](https://microsoft.github.io/autogen/) — Framework for building multi-agent conversational systems.
- [Lilian Weng: LLM Powered Autonomous Agents](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2023-06-23-agent/) — Comprehensive technical overview of agent architectures.

## Evaluation & Testing

- [Hamel Husain: Your AI Product Needs Evals](https://hamel.dev/blog/posts/evals/) — Practical guide to building evaluation systems for AI products.
- [Braintrust AI](https://www.braintrust.dev/) — Platform for evaluating and improving AI applications.
- [Anthropic: Evaluating AI Systems](https://docs.anthropic.com/en/docs/build-with-claude/develop-tests) — Guide to testing and evaluating Claude-based systems.
- [Shreya Shankar: Rethinking LLM Evaluation](https://www.sh-reya.com/) — Research on practical evaluation methodology.

## Due Diligence & Vendor Assessment

- [Bessemer Venture Partners: State of the Cloud](https://www.bvp.com/atlas/state-of-the-cloud-2024) — Useful benchmarking data for evaluating AI/SaaS businesses.
- [CB Insights: AI 100](https://www.cbinsights.com/research/report/artificial-intelligence-top-startups-2024/) — Annual list of promising AI companies, useful for competitive landscape analysis.

## Building Internal Capability

- [Google: Machine Learning Crash Course](https://developers.google.com/machine-learning/crash-course) — Free, comprehensive introduction to ML fundamentals.
- [Fast.ai: Practical Deep Learning for Coders](https://www.fast.ai/) — Top-down, practical approach to learning deep learning.
- [Andrew Ng: AI for Everyone (Coursera)](https://www.coursera.org/learn/ai-for-everyone) — Non-technical introduction to AI for business leaders.
- [Chip Huyen: Designing Machine Learning Systems](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/designing-machine-learning/9781098107956/) — Essential reading on production ML system design.
- [Made With ML](https://madewithml.com/) — End-to-end MLOps curriculum.

## MLOps & Production Infrastructure

- [Google: MLOps Maturity Model](https://cloud.google.com/architecture/mlops-continuous-delivery-and-automation-pipelines-in-machine-learning) — Framework for assessing production ML infrastructure maturity.
- [Weights & Biases: Effective MLOps](https://wandb.ai/site/articles) — Practical articles on experiment tracking, model management, and deployment.
- [The MLOps Community](https://mlops.community/) — Active community of practitioners sharing operational knowledge.
- [Chip Huyen: Real-time Machine Learning](https://huyenchip.com/2022/01/02/real-time-machine-learning-challenges-and-solutions.html) — Challenges and solutions for real-time ML serving.

## Responsible AI & Governance

- [NIST AI Risk Management Framework](https://www.nist.gov/artificial-intelligence/executive-order-safe-secure-and-trustworthy-artificial-intelligence) — US government framework for managing AI risks.
- [EU AI Act: Official Text](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/) — Full text and guides to the EU's AI regulation.
- [Anthropic: Core Views on AI Safety](https://www.anthropic.com/research) — Perspectives on responsible AI development from a leading lab.
- [Montreal AI Ethics Institute](https://montrealethics.ai/) — Research and resources on ethical AI deployment.
- [UK AI Safety Institute](https://www.aisi.gov.uk/) — UK government body for AI safety research and evaluation.

## Communities & Newsletters

- [AI Engineering (Latent Space)](https://www.latent.space/) — Newsletter and podcast covering AI engineering.
- [The Batch (DeepLearning.AI)](https://www.deeplearning.ai/the-batch/) — Andrew Ng's weekly AI newsletter.

- [Ahead of AI (Sebastian Raschka)](https://magazine.sebastianraschka.com/) — Deep technical analysis of new research.

Found a resource that should be on this list? Open an issue or submit a pull request. We're looking for resources that are grounded in operational reality rather than theoretical or vendor-driven.

**Criteria for inclusion:**
- Written by practitioners with hands-on experience
- Provides actionable guidance, not just thought leadership
- Maintained and reasonably current
- Free or has meaningful free content available
